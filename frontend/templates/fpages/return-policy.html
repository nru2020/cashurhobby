Return and cancellation Policy

1. All products can be returned within 7 days of product delivery. Please ensure that the product is unused and with all tags intact.

2. Requests for return must done online by visiting your Account page - Here is url->……………………………………….. (we can procedure here)

3. We have free reverse pick up option available at most pincodes. However, in case the pincode is not serviceable due to any reason, we may ask you to courier us back the product and share the tracking slip with us. The charges for return in this case would not be reimbursed.

4. You could choose to seek a full refund in the form of redeemable store credits (which you can use later to shop at cashurhobby) or refund on your original credit / debit card or bank account.

5. Refund via your original credit / debit card or bank account shall be initiated after receiving back the product. And the credit shall be done within 7 days of the same. In case you choose store credits as a mode of refund, the same shall be issued within 48 hours of the logistics partner confirming us about a successful pick up. Store Credits have a validity period of 6 months.

6. In case of discounts applied during ordering, the discount value shall also be adjusted in the refund, as applicable.

7. COD collection charges on COD orders are non-refundable.
